How It Works
Voting Fans rank up to 8 wrestlers per weight class, top 12 Pound-for-Pound, and top 8 teams each week. Scoring Weight classes: 1st = 8 pts down to 8th = 1 pt. P4P: 1st = 12 pts down to 12th = 1 pt. Teams: 1st = 8 pts down to 8th = 1 pt. Updates Rankings refresh live as new votes come in. A new ballot opens each week of the season. Off-Season Votes cast now count toward Week 1 when the season opens in November.
