How It Works
Voting Fans rank up to 8 wrestlers per weight class each week. Scoring 1st place = 8 pts, 2nd = 7 pts, down to 8th = 1 pt. Rankings reflect weighted totals across all ballots. Updates Rankings refresh live as new votes come in. A new ballot opens each week of the season. Off-Season Votes cast now count toward Week 1 when the season opens in November.
